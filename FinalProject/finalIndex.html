WELCOME TO GUARDIAN HOSPICE

Guardian Hospice offers a full service hospice care program through a staff of highly experienced, compassionate, and dedicated professionals. We provide expert care and counseling to our families and their loved ones. We know that each patient is unique and our smaller, personalized care team focuses on individualized patient and family care. The Guardian Hospice Team is dedicated to providing the best care for our patients and their extended families. Our compassionate professionals understand that we are caring for not only our patients, but their loved ones as well. The team is comprised of the following:

  • Patient's Physician
  • Hospice Medical Director
  • Registered Nurse
  • Social Worker
  • Certified Nursing Assistant
  • Spiritual Care Coordinator
  • Bereavement Coordinator
  • Volunteers
